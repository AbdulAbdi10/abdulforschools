About Abdul Abdi

Abdul Abdi serves as the Minneapolis Public Schools Board Director for District 1, representing the Northeast and Southeast communities. As a parent, School Board Director, and senior IT architect, Abdul brings practical leadership grounded in equity, transparency, and student-centered decision-making.

With deep family and community roots in Minneapolis, Abdul has worked to strengthen schools, uplift families, and ensure that every student has access to high-quality education and strong pathways for success.

Priorities & Issues

Academic Achievement

Strengthening literacy, math, early intervention, and instruction to improve academic results for all students.

Safe & Supportive Schools

Improving school climate, enhancing mental health supports, and ensuring every school is a safe place for students to thrive.

Family Engagement

Building stronger partnerships with parents, improving communication, and elevating family voices across District 1.

Budget Transparency

Ensuring responsible oversight of district resources so more funding reaches classrooms and student support services.

Accomplishments

  • District 1 Improvements: Strengthened resources for Northeast and Southeast schools through active advocacy and collaboration.
  • Budget Leadership: Improved fiscal transparency and oversight as Chair of the Finance Committee.
  • CTE Pathway Expansion: Supported expanding career and technical education at Edison High School.
  • Middle School Arts & Music: Championed arts access and programming at Northeast Middle School.
  • Student-Centered Decisions: Prioritized equitable resources, academic needs, and student success across MPS.
