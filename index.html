About Abdul Abdi

Abdul Abdi is a Minneapolis Public Schools Board Director representing District 1. As a parent, community leader, and senior IT architect, he brings a commitment to equity, transparency, and student-centered decision-making.

Abdul has deep roots in Northeast and Southeast Minneapolis—supporting neighborhood schools, engaging families, and building partnerships that strengthen the district. His work centers on ensuring all students have access to high-quality education, safe learning environments, and pathways to success.

Accomplishments

  • District 1 Wins: Supported schools across Northeast & Southeast with improved resources and family partnerships.
  • Budget Leadership: Strengthened financial oversight and transparency as Chair of the Finance Committee.
  • CTE Expansion: Advocated for stronger Career & Technical Education pathways at Edison High School.
  • Middle School Arts: Championed music and arts access for Northeast Middle School and beyond.
  • Student-Centered Decisions: Prioritized academic outcomes and equitable resource distribution.
